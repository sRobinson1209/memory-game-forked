Game Instructions:

Number Game Survival Mode:

Player is shown a sequence of numbers. Player then must try to remember the numbers and enter them in the same order they were shown. If the player enters the numbers incorrectly, then the game will be over, and no points will be awarded. Otherwise the player proceeds through the game. The game will go on infinitely with each sequence of numbers becoming longer and faster. Every 7 levels, the sequence will get longer and longer. The player is also awarded 10 points for each level completed correctly.

Number Game Relaxed Mode:

Player is shown a sequence of numbers.Player must try to remember the numbers and enter them in the same order they were shown. If the player enters the numbers incorrectly, they will still have a chance to enter the numbers or skip. The game only ends if the player chooses to quit the game. The game will go on infinitely with each sequence of numbers becoming longer and faster. The player will be rewarded 5 points for each level completed.

Rhythm Game

The user is given a beat to follow demonstrated by the rippling rings in the circle. Once the white rings are done, green rings are shown which is the same rhythm as the white rings. Using the spacebar, the player should repeat the rhythm as closley as possible.

Melody Game

The user is given three notes initially to listen to and then repeat back to the game.First the game will play the melody to play bacl. Then the user will push 'start listening' to allow the game to listen for input. Once they finish the sequence they will press stop listening to lock in their guess.
